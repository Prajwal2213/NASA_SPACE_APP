Focusing data for a

Greener

tomorrow.

EcoLens is a team of passionate storytellers, data scientists, and environmental advocates committed to bringing climate data into clear focus for the public. Using the powerful datasets of the US Greenhouse Gas Center, EcoLens crafts compelling narratives that transform complex scientific data into accessible, engaging, and actionable insights. By visualizing the impact of greenhouse gases and highlighting solutions, EcoLens aims to educate and inspire communities to take meaningful climate action.
